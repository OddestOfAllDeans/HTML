"Every bite is a memory to cherish for a lifetime"

Embark on a culinary adventure with Chiny, where every bite tells a tale of daring and discovery! Founded by the intrepid Muhammad Ali Dean Baskoro Ajie, Chiny's story is one of extraordinary quests and flavorful conquests. Inspired by a journey through the lush forests of Indonesia, Muhammad Ali stumbled upon a hidden treasure: a rare, golden honey with a taste so exquisite, it sparked an idea that would change the fried chicken game forever. Driven by his passion for gastronomic innovation, Muhammad Ali embarked on a daring quest to perfect his honey glazed fried chicken recipe. He traversed mountains, crossed rivers, and braved the untamed wilderness in search of the finest ingredients. His relentless pursuit of perfection led him to combine the succulent crispiness of fried chicken with the irresistible sweetness of honey, creating a harmonious symphony of flavors that would captivate taste buds far and wide. Today, Chiny stands as a testament to Muhammad's fearless spirit and unwavering dedication to his craft. Each piece of our honey glazed fried chicken is a tribute to his adventurous soul, inviting you to experience a taste of his extraordinary journey. Join us at Chiny and embark on your own culinary adventure, where every bite is a memory to cherish for a lifetime.
